# 🎯 Interview Strategy

### 📌 Why?

A structured interview approach improves performance.

---

### Recommended Flow

1. Clarify the problem.
2. Ask questions.
3. Discuss brute-force.
4. Optimize.
5. Explain complexity.
6. Write clean code.
7. Test edge cases.

---

### 🌍 Real-Life Example

Planning a road trip before starting the journey.

---

### 🧠 DSA Connection

🧠 Direct

Problem-solving process.

---

### ⚡ One-Line Interview Answer

# ⚠ Common Mistakes

### Avoid:

* Jumping into coding immediately.
* Ignoring edge cases.
* Not discussing complexity.
* Staying silent while thinking.
* Overcomplicating simple solutions.
* Giving vague behavioral answers.
* Memorizing instead of understanding.

---

### ⚡ One-Line Interview Answer

Interview success depends as much on communication and structure as on technical correctness.
